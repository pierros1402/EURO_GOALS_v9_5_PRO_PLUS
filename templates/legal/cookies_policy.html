Cookies Policy

This Cookies Policy explains how AI MatchLab ("we", "our", "the platform") uses cookies and similar technologies in accordance with the EU ePrivacy Directive and GDPR requirements.

1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you access websites or online services. They allow platforms to operate correctly, remember user preferences, and improve performance.

2. Types of Cookies We Use

AI MatchLab uses the following categories of cookies:

Essential Cookies

These cookies are strictly necessary for the platform’s core functionality, including navigation, security, session handling, and loading essential resources. They **cannot** be disabled.

Functional Cookies

These cookies allow the platform to remember preferences such as theme mode, layout settings, and language.

Analytics Cookies

We may use anonymous, aggregated analytics to understand usage patterns and improve performance. No personally identifiable information is collected.

No Advertising Cookies

AI MatchLab does not use marketing, third-party advertising, or tracking cookies.

3. Cookies Used by Our Platform

  • session_id – maintains basic session integrity.
  • theme_mode – stores light/dark mode preference.
  • pwa_install_prompt – remembers PWA installation state.
  • layout_settings – saves user layout customizations.

4. Managing Cookies

You can manage or delete cookies at any time through your browser settings:

  • Clear browsing data
  • Block or allow cookies
  • Adjust privacy & tracking settings

However, disabling essential cookies may affect platform functionality.

5. Legal Basis

Essential cookies are processed under legitimate interest. Functional and analytics cookies may require user consent depending on local regulations.

6. Updates to This Policy

This Cookies Policy may be updated to reflect regulatory changes or improvements to the platform’s technology.

This document complies with GDPR and the EU ePrivacy Directive.
