Responsible Gambling

At AI MatchLab, we are committed to promoting safe, responsible, and controlled engagement with sports data, statistics, and betting-related information. While our platform provides live market intelligence, it is essential that users understand the risks associated with betting activities and maintain full personal responsibility.

1. Our Commitment

AI MatchLab follows the principles of the UK Gambling Commission (UKGC), the Malta Gaming Authority (MGA), and the European Gaming & Betting Association (EGBA). We support a safe, transparent, and harm-free digital environment.

2. Maintaining Control

Gambling should always be a form of entertainment. To ensure you remain in control, consider the following guidelines:

  • Set a financial limit and never exceed it.
  • Bet only with money you can afford to lose.
  • Never chase losses.
  • Avoid gambling when stressed, tired, or under emotional pressure.
  • Keep regular breaks and avoid long sessions.

3. Recognizing Problematic Behaviour

  • Spending more money or time than intended.
  • Borrowing money to place bets.
  • Feeling stress, frustration, or sadness due to betting outcomes.
  • Difficulty stopping or reducing betting activity.
  • Neglecting work, family, or personal responsibilities.

4. Self-Exclusion & Protection Tools

If you believe your betting habits are becoming problematic, consider the following tools:

  • Deposit limits – set a maximum amount you are willing to spend.
  • Time-outs – take temporary breaks.
  • Self-exclusion – restrict access to betting platforms.
  • Gambling blockers such as Gamban or BetBlocker.

5. Professional Support & Assistance

If you need help, please contact one of the official support organizations:

  • BeGambleAware
  • GamCare
  • GamblingTherapy
  • GAMSTOP
