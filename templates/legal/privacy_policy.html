Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy explains how AI MatchLab ("we", "our", "the platform") collects, processes, stores, and protects your personal data in accordance with the General Data Protection Regulation (EU 2016/679) and applicable European privacy laws.

1. Data Controller

The data controller responsible for processing your information is AI MatchLab. If you have any questions about this privacy policy, you may contact us at:

Email: support@aimatchlab.com

2. Information We Collect

We may collect the following categories of data:

  • Account information: email, username, encrypted authentication tokens.
  • Usage data: pages visited, platform interactions, session duration.
  • Device data: browser, operating system, IP address (pseudonymized).
  • Cookies & tracking data: technical cookies required for platform operation.
  • API/Worker interaction logs: internal system data for performance and debugging.

3. How We Use Your Data

Your data may be processed for the following purposes:

  • To provide access to the AI MatchLab platform and its services.
  • To ensure platform security and prevent abuse or fraudulent activity.
  • To analyze performance, improve our systems, and optimize user experience.
  • To comply with regulatory, legal, or security obligations.

4. Legal Basis for Processing (GDPR)

We process data under the following legal bases:

  • Contractual necessity → enabling access and service functionality.
  • Legitimate interest → platform security, analytics, service optimization.
  • Legal obligation → compliance with EU data protection regulations.
  • Consent → where cookie consent applies.

5. Cookies & Tracking

AI MatchLab uses technical cookies essential for platform function. We do not use marketing cookies or third-party advertising trackers. Detailed information is provided in our Cookies Policy.

6. Data Retention

We store personal data only for as long as necessary to provide our services or comply with legal obligations. Usage logs may be retained for a maximum of 12 months for security and debugging purposes.

7. Data Sharing & Transfers

We do not sell, rent, or share personal data with third-party advertisers. Data may be shared only with:

  • Infrastructure providers (hosting, cloud services)
  • Security & anti-fraud partners
  • Regulatory authorities if legally required

Whenever data is transferred outside the EU, we ensure GDPR-compliant safeguards (SCCs).

8. Your Rights Under GDPR

You have the following rights:

  • Right to access your data
  • Right to rectification
  • Right to erasure (“right to be forgotten”)
  • Right to restrict processing
  • Right to object
  • Right to data portability

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at: support@aimatchlab.com

9. Security Measures

We apply industry-standard protections including encryption, secure transport (HTTPS), server isolation, role-based access controls, and continuous monitoring.

10. Updates to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy to reflect legal or technical changes. The latest version will always be available on this page.

This Privacy Policy complies with GDPR, UKGC guidelines, and EU ePrivacy standards.
